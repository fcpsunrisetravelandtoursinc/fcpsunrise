COMPANY PROFILE

FCP SUNRISE TRAVEL AND TOURS INC. started its first mall branch in 2019. We are among the ranks of the most reputable and reliable travel agencies in the Philippines. We are led by our CEO with 18 years experience in the industry.

FCP Sunrise Travel and Tours Inc. is located at Checkpoint Mall Calamba City, SM Supermarket SM City Calamba and SM City Sto Tomas.

Major corporations, government organizations, business groups and solo travelers have entrusted their domestic and international travel arrangements with us including arrangements for business conventions, trainings, incentive travel and personal leisure or vacation travel for groups and individuals.

To ensure that our clients and customers get the best offers and deals, we have partnered with major hotels, resorts, transportation companies and reputable airlines.

True to our vision and mission as a reliable, customer-centric travel company, FCP Sunrise Travel and Tours Inc. has successfully made a name among its satisfied and loyal customers as their travel partner of choice for all seasons.
